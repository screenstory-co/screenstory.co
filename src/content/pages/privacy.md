# Privacy Policy Summary – Screen Story Co.

**Source:** screenstory.co/privacy.html  
**Last Updated:** 20/02/2024

---

## Scope & Commitment

- Screen Story Co. is bound by the **Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)**.
- The policy applies to all customers and visitors of the website.
- They reserve the right to alter the policy; updates will be posted on the site.
- They take no responsibility for the privacy practices of external sites linked from their site.

---

## Information Collected

| When Collected | What is Collected |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Ordering / registering | Name, email address, credit card information, and other details needed for the transaction. |
| Subscribing to newsletter, responding to surveys, filling out forms | As appropriate for the interaction. |

> “When ordering or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, credit card information or other details to help you with your experience.”

---

## How Information is Used

- To personalise user experience and deliver relevant content/products.
- To respond to customer service requests.
- To process transactions quickly.
- To send periodic emails about orders or other products/services.

---

## Security Measures

- **No PCI vulnerability scanning** – but regular Malware Scanning is performed.
- Personal information is stored behind **secured networks** with limited access.
- Sensitive/credit information is encrypted via **SSL technology**.
- All transactions are processed through a **third-party gateway provider**; data is **not stored or processed on their servers**.

---

## Cookies

- Used to remember shopping cart items, user preferences, track advertisements, and compile aggregate site traffic data.
- Third-party services may also track this data.
- Users can disable cookies via browser settings, but some site features may not function properly.

---

## Third-Party Disclosure

> “We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information unless we provide you with advance notice.”

Exceptions:
- Website hosting partners and other parties assisting in operations (must agree to confidentiality).
- When required by law, to enforce site policies, or to protect rights/property/safety.

**Non-personally identifiable information** may be shared for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

---

## Third-Party Links

- Third-party products/services may appear on the site. Those sites have independent privacy policies. Screen Story Co. assumes no responsibility for them but welcomes feedback on any linked sites.

---

## Email Correspondence

Email addresses are collected to:
- Send information, respond to inquiries, process orders and order updates.
- Send additional product/service information.
- Market to mailing list or continue emails after original transaction.

**Compliance commitments:**
- No false or misleading subjects/addresses.
- Identify advertisements as such.
- Include physical business address.
- Monitor third-party email marketing services for compliance.
- Honour opt-out/unsubscribe requests promptly – users can unsubscribe via the link at the bottom of each email.
