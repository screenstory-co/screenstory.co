SEO defined as "the organic and customer centric marketing strategy focused on making your website more relevant and targeted to potential customers."

## Five Pillars

- **Keyword Research** – identify terms customers use.
- **On-Page SEO** – Title tags, meta descriptions, headings (H1–H3), clean URLs, content quality, internal linking.
- **Off-Page SEO** – Backlink building, social media marketing.
- **Technical SEO** – Site speed, mobile-friendliness, XML sitemaps, robots.txt.
- **User Experience** – Easy navigation, responsive design, readable content.

Their Weebly-based CMS covers many SEO elements by default.
