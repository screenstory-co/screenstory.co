## Three Considerations

1. **Geography** – Use country-specific (.co.uk, .com.au) or regional (.london, .melbourne) domains for local relevance.
2. **Industry / Specialisation** – New TLDs like .fitness, .estate, .house can highlight focus.
3. **Just Do It!** – A .com is not required for success. Don't let unavailability stop you.

> "Acquiring a .COM domain is not a prerequisite for online success."
